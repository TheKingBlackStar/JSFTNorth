New to JSTF North?

We're thrilled you're considering a visit! At JSTF North, you'll find a warm, informal atmosphere where everyone is welcome. We focus on the Apostle's Doctrine and sharing the complete truth of Jesus Christ.

  • Service Times: Join us! (Mon-Wed 7pm, Fri 7pm, Sun 9am & 7pm). Check our Events Calendar for more.
  • Location: 1400 Taylor St, Sandusky Ohio, 44870. Get Directions.
