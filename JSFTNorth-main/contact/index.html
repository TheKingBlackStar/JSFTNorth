What to Expect

Visiting a new church can sometimes feel daunting, but we want you to feel right at home at JSTF North! Expect a welcoming and informal atmosphere, heartfelt worship, prayer, and teaching centered on the Apostle's Doctrine. Come as you are—there's no formal dress code. We are family-friendly and aim to provide an environment where children can also learn and enjoy.
